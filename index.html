ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

Who We Are

For over a decade the Inspiring Youths Africa Initiative and Inspiring Youths Africa Magazine an initiative and publication of RestLogic Nigeria is committed to identifying, inspiring, equipping and celebrating the very best of young leaders in all spheres of life in Nigeria, Africa and the world at large. We inspire faith, equip with the right knowledge and empower young leaders with information needed to grow.

What We Do

We raise the next generation of independent, vibrant, excellent and exceptional young leaders in all fields of human endeavors who in turn impact their communities from Africa to the rest of the world. We drive the initiative through, community engagements, stakeholderâ€™s enlightenment, advocacy, civil discourse and dialogue. We constructively engage government and stakeholders alike to achieve visible and meaningful results.

Our Objectives

Discover, Empower and raise inspiring leaders that will be role models of change to the young and upcoming generation.

Promote Leaderships awareness at all levels to empower, prepare and produce the next generation of Leaders with the right skillset.

Support and ensure the emergence of vibrant, competent and excellent leaders at all levels to reinforce the status of Africa.

Ensure active Youths and Women participation in Leadership, Governance, Policy formulation and implementation.

Advocate for equality, Justice and Fairness for all Africans especially the youths.

Promote skills and talent education for maximum productivity.
